💊 Daily Medication Schedule — Every Day

⏰ 07:30 AM — With Breakfast
Livazo (Pitavastatin) 2 mg
Take with breakfast. Unlike other statins, can be taken at any time of day. Metabolically neutral — does not worsen glucose or insulin resistance. Monitor LFTs at 12 weeks.
⏰ 07:30 AM — With Breakfast
Losartan (for hypertension)
Take alongside Livazo with food and a full glass of water. Renoprotective in diabetes. Monitor BP daily, potassium every 3 months.
⏰ Once Weekly — After Breakfast
Mounjaro (Tirzepatide) 2.5 mg SC
Inject subcutaneously (abdomen, thigh, or upper arm). Rotate sites. Same day each week. Weeks 1–6: 2.5mg → then increase to 5mg per endocrinologist plan.
📅 From Month 2 — Morning
+ Jardiance (Empagliflozin) 10 mg
With breakfast. Renal + cardiovascular protection. Monitor for UTI symptoms and hydration. Ensure adequate fluid intake.
📅 From Month 2 — With Dinner
+ Glucophage XR (Metformin) 500 mg
Take with dinner to minimize GI side effects. Extended-release form reduces nausea. Increases gradually as tolerated.
🌙 Every Night
CPAP Machine
Non-negotiable. Full night use every night including naps. Optimal CPAP compliance directly reduces erythrocytosis and improves insulin sensitivity.
